Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference.

What happens after the trial?

After your 14-day Pro trial, you can continue with a paid plan or downgrade to Starter (free) to keep using basic features.

Is Ghost Protocol really 100% undetectable?

Ghost Protocol v2 has been tested against all major bot detection systems. While we can't guarantee 100% in all scenarios, our success rate is consistently above 99.5%.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes! If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we offer a full refund, no questions asked.

What browsers are supported?

QAntum supports Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Ghost Protocol works best with Chrome-based browsers.

Can I self-host QAntum?

Enterprise plans include on-premise deployment options. Contact our sales team for details on self-hosted solutions.
